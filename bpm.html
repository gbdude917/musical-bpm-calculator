What is BPM?

Beats per minute (BPM) is a musical term to denote the tempo for a song. This means that a certain type of musical note value will be assigned one beat. The number that the website will find is how many beats occur in a minute.

How to Use

  1. Choose the song you wish to find the BPM of.
  2. Click the button to the beat of the song.

Note: the longer you keep tapping the button, the more accurate the calculation will be.
